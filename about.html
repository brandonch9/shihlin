About

Thousands of Taiwanese crowd the local night markets in search of their favorite delicious snacks. Taipei's Shihlin Night Market is the most famous due to its wide range of local delicacies in its small streets and alleways. If a snack is local favorite, you can surely find it in Shihlin! As the locals say, “If Taiwan has it, Shihlin has it.” At Shihlin Taiwanese Street Snacks, our aim is to give everybody the opportunity to do the same and enjoy the wonderful street snacks of Taiwan!
