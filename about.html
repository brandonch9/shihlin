Step into the
world of shihlin

Established in 2004, Shihlin Taiwan Street Snacks (R) brings delicious dishes found in the famous street markets in Taiwan to over 150 locations in Asia.

Prepared just the way they were done in Taiwan, our menu features authentic and popular dishes such as XXL Crispy chicken, Handmade Oyster Mee Sua, Plum Fries and more.

In 2016, Shihlin has established its first store in Milpitas, California, USA. More stores are planned throughout California in the coming months and years.
