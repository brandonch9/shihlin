Authentic Taiwanese Snacks

Shihlin Snacks is everybody’s favorite Taiwanese food chain featuring popular snacks from the alleys of Taiwan’s night markets. Prepared the way they do over in Taiwan, the menu features authentic favorites such as XXL Crispy Chicken, Crispy Floss Egg Crepe, Handmade Oyster Mee Sua and Seafood Tempura.

Milpitas Grand Opening

Shihlin Snacks will open to customers on December 17, 2017. We are located next to Fantasia Coffee & Tea in Milpitas Square.
